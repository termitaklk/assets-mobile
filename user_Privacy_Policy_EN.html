Introduction

    Welcome to YGOMobile (hereinafter referred to as "this app").
    We take your privacy very seriously and are committed to protecting your personal information.
    This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use this app. Please read carefully.

Information Collection and Usage

  • Access Network Information
    Used for various functions to connect to the network. Can be disabled.
  • Read External Storage
    This permission is used to read images from the gallery for modifying card backs, backgrounds, avatars, and restoring local backups of decks. Can be disabled.
  • Write External Storage
    This permission is used for backing up decks to external storage. Can be disabled.
  • Clipboard Content
    This app may read clipboard content but only within the scope necessary for enhancing user experience.
    For example, copied deck codes, battle room codes.
    We commit not to use such information for any other purposes. Can be disabled.
  • Invoke App
    This app allows users to choose to invoke it from other apps but only for importing external files by selecting this app as an open method. Can be disabled.
  • Personal Information Usage
    When you register for a Mengka platform account, we will ask for some basic information such as username, email address, etc. We commit not to use such information for any other purposes.
  • Information Sharing and Disclosure
    Without your consent, we will not sell your personal information to third parties.
    However, under legal requirements, we may share your information.
  • Security Assurance
    We employ various technical measures to ensure the security of your personal information and prevent data loss, misuse, or unauthorized access.
    Nevertheless, please understand that no transmission method over the internet is 100% secure.
  • Your Rights
    According to applicable laws, you have the right to request access, correction, deletion of your personal information, and withdrawal of consent for our processing of your personal information.
